Smart Robot for Office Document and Parcel Delivery

My goal is to enable users to effectively monitor and control the activities of the robot, ensuring the accurate delivery of documents or parcels to their intended destinations. I aim to develop a user-friendly interface that facilitates the selection of predefined drop-off and pick-up points. This interface will simplify the process for employees, making it more efficient to manage deliveries. By focusing on usability, I hope to enhance the overall user experience. Ultimately, my goal is to streamline operations and improve the reliability of document and parcel delivery.
